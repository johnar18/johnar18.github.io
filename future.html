forever after as an alum. What then?

It’s our philosophy that the quality of a student’s undergraduate experience directly affects their alumni experience. This is why we start before day one of their Carolina journey to help create a positive experience for Carolina students — past, present and future.

As you probably know, Carolina students go places

  • As of last year, 92% of Carolina graduates were employed or admitted to graduate school.
  • Carolina students have also been awarded the Rhodes Scholarship 48 times.
  • 17 Carolina students have achieved the Churchill Scholarship from the University of Cambridge.
  • Hundreds of Carolina graduates go on to play professional sports.
  • UNC's Career Services offers 30,000 internships to help you build connections for your bright future.
