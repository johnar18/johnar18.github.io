UNC, Basically

A site by current students for future students.

It's not what's found here. It's who.
Carolina's just for you.

Pictures don’t do Carolina justice, and neither do UNC's official websites. There’s a sense of being here, a feeling that’s impossible to fully describe. You know it as soon as you walk onto campus, but the people in charge here won't tell you what that is. They don't know what it is because they just run the place. But, we live here, we breathe here, and we strive to thrive with Carolina Blue in our veins. This is our school, and if you're reading this, it's too late then you should be a part of it, too.

Take a moment to look around and see why.

Academics

Science, humanities, professional school, or anything in between, there is a place and a degree for you. We aren't considered a "public ivy" for nothing.

Athletics

Who doesn't balls and people being thrown around for points and entertainment? There's a rumor we're pretty good at basketball or something, but that's probably a lie.

Clubs and Organizations

There are a lot of places with even more perspectives within them, and there is bound to be one just for you. Find your people, and express yourself.

Housing

Sharing a suite, friends down the hall, or an apartment of your own, create a place you can and should call home, just a walk away from everything you need.

Traditions and History

Styling and profiling since 1789, we're home to many "firsts" and "bests" in history. Whether you make them or celebrate them, you can become a part of them, too.

Preparation for the Future

Whether it's two years or ten, everyone here makes their mark and pays their dues to then leave for greater pastures, and Carolina builds a great base for making that transition easy.
